# Medium Wave 01 Devenira

This is the first real Medium distribution wave based on the owned-site blog inventory now live inside Devenira.

Core objective:

- make the Medium profile feel like a coherent world
- prove the founder voice fast
- let readers understand the problem before they understand the product
- route trust back toward the owned site and Devenira over time

## Operating Rule

- owned site publishes first
- Medium republishes after a short delay
- Medium should feel like a readable destination, not a disguised landing page

## Why These 6 Go First

The first Medium wave should answer:

`Why should I keep reading this person instead of every other diet writer?`

The answer should be:

- this person understands the humiliating private logic of dieting
- this person writes from lived experience
- this feed has a worldview, not random advice
