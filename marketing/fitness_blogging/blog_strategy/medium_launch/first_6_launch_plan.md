# First 6 Medium Launch Plan

## Launch Logic

The first Medium wave should answer this question for a new reader:

**“Why should I keep reading this person instead of every other diet writer?”**

The answer is not:

- more science
- more motivation
- more fitness clichés

The answer is:

- this person understands the humiliating private logic of dieting
- this person writes like someone who has actually lived it
- this feed feels like a world, not random advice

## Recommended Posting Rhythm

Suggested rhythm:

- `Monday`
- `Wednesday`
- `Friday`

That gives each post room to breathe while still building momentum.

## What To Publish In Week 3

If the first two weeks feel healthy, publish next:

- `opening_run_23_post_023_en.md`
- `opening_run_18_post_186_en.md`
- `opening_run_10_post_306_en.md`

This gives you:

- one personal proof / milestone post
- one emotional food-preference post
- one practical food-structure post

## Important Launch Rule

Do not make the first six posts all look the same.

Use this mix:

- `2` real-photo covers
- `4` AI / conceptual covers

That balance makes the feed feel:

- credible
- personal
- intentional

without turning into endless transformation wallpaper.
