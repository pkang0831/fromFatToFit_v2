## Caption

The mirror is not useless.

It is just bad at telling the truth in real time.

When you see your body every day, small changes stop feeling like changes.

That is why people can be doing the work and still feel like nothing is happening.

The mirror shows one emotional frame.

It does not show:

- context
- trend
- comparison

That is why better evidence matters.

A weekly visual record is harder to distort than one bathroom-mirror mood check.

Proof over panic.
