- 대학교 동기
- 가족/친구

- 이유: LinkedIn 알고리즘이 "2nd/3rd degree" 네트워크 기반으로 피드 추천. 1st degree connections 많을수록 glass ceiling 뚫림.

LinkedIn 검색창에서 아래 키워드로 검색 → Connect 요청 (짧은 메시지 꼭 첨부):

- "sports nutritionist"
- "health writer"
- "founder health tech"

## 5. Short-form observation post 템플릿 (Fri용)

장문 아닌 짧은 관찰 글. 이게 사실 LinkedIn에서 reach가 더 잘 나옴.

```
The mirror lies more than the scale does.

The scale has a number. The mirror has a mood.

The scale is wrong in predictable ways: water, glycogen, sodium,
time of day. You can correct for those.

The mirror is wrong in unpredictable ways: lighting, yesterday's
sleep, today's stress, the last thing you ate, the frame you came
in with.

One is a bad instrument. The other is not an instrument at all.

Trust the weekly photo. Trust the clothes. Trust the tape.
The mirror is the last voice to listen to during a transformation.
```

이런 식으로 200-350자. 아침에 바로 쓰고 바로 포스팅. 생각 너무 하지 말기.
