### Comment template (paste, then customize)

For a "scale went up" question:

```
Yeah — this happens, and it almost never means what people think it means.

Body weight is a crowded number. It includes water, glycogen, food still 
moving through digestion, sodium, and hormonal water shifts. A 1-2 kg 
overnight rise after eating less is almost always one of those, not new fat.

Fat doesn't arrive that fast. The math just doesn't work.

The thing that fixed this for me was: stop reading the morning. Read the 
week. Same time, same conditions, weekly average. That's the only number 
that gives a real signal.
```

### Rules

- Don't comment on multiple threads in one session (looks spammy → mods notice)
- Don't post a self-promo thread; only comment on others' questions
- If your comment gets downvoted, leave it; don't delete or argue

## Action B: Hacker News submission (~10 min prep, then post Tue 7am PT)

This works ONLY for the founder story (`wave_01_02`). Other posts will sink.

### Pre-flight

Confirm wave_01_02 is published on Medium. You'll need its URL.

### Submission template

| Option | Why |
|--------|-----|

| **The slow middle is where most diets quit** | Insight-led, hooks curious |

URL: paste your Medium URL

### Post immediately after submission (CRITICAL — first comment)

Without a first comment from the author, HN posts almost never make front page. Comment within 60 seconds of submitting:

```
Author here.

I built Devenira (https://devenira.com) around exactly the gap this 
post documents — the unphotographable middle of body change where the 
work is real but the mirror isn't reassuring.

Two things I almost cut from the post:

1. The sub-three-month period was the worst, not the start. I'd been 
prepared for hunger and fatigue. I wasn't prepared for the part where 
the scale stopped being generous and the mirror started lying.

2. I almost quit at month four. The thing that kept me in was a weekly 
photo I'd been taking, side by side with the previous month. The body 
change wasn't in any one frame. It was in the comparison.

Happy to answer questions on the program structure, body image, or 
the maintenance phase that nobody talks about.
```

### Best submission window

- **Tuesday or Wednesday or Thursday**
- **7:00–9:00 AM Pacific Time** (= 10:00–12:00 PM Eastern, 4:00–6:00 PM London, 12:00–14:00 AM Korea)
- AVOID: weekends, Mondays (firehose), holidays

### After submitting

- Watch the first 30 minutes — if it gets 5+ upvotes within 30 min, you're on /new and might break /front
- If comments come, respond honestly, especially to skeptics — HN rewards good-faith engagement
- DO NOT ask people to upvote (HN bans for vote rings)
