# Founder Story Post Asset Sequence

This file maps the shortlisted images into a concrete founder story visual order.

Goal:

- make the founder story feel true
- show a real before
- show the hard middle
- prove the change happened in stages
- end with a more composed founder presence

Reference strategy:

- `marketing/fitness_blogging/blog_strategy/devenirakang_launch/post_001_founder_story_carousel.md`
- `marketing/devenira_prelaunch/content_engine_2week.md`

## Best 7-Slide Founder Story Sequence

### Slide 1: Hook / transformed present
- Asset: `marketing/fitness_blogging/my_past_photos/extracted/Takeout/Google Photos/Photos from 2025/20250920_193842.jpg`
- Role:
  - stop the scroll
  - show unmistakable proof of change
- Why this first:
  - it earns attention immediately
  - visible leanness is strong enough to carry the opening hook

Suggested headline:

`Built for the week you want to quit.`

Suggested sub:

`The mirror did not show this all at once.`

### Slide 2: Real before
- Asset: `marketing/fitness_blogging/my_past_photos/extracted/Takeout/Google Photos/Photos from 2024/IMG_1240.JPG`
- Role:
  - remove any suspicion that the story is vague or inflated
  - show the honest starting point

Suggested headline:

`This is where the story actually starts.`

Suggested sub:

`No lighting. No angles. No momentum.`

### Slide 3: The middle
- Asset: `marketing/fitness_blogging/my_past_photos/extracted/Takeout/Google Photos/Photos from 2025/20250716_064034.jpg`
- Role:
  - show a realistic “ordinary middle” check-in
  - connect directly to the product idea

Suggested headline:

Suggested sub:

`This is the stretch where people start thinking nothing is happening.`

### Slide 4: Progress became visible

- Role:
  - prove that change happened before the final reveal

Suggested headline:

Suggested sub:

`The work became real before it looked dramatic.`

### Slide 5: Two-angle check-in / proof logic
- Asset A: `marketing/fitness_blogging/my_past_photos/extracted/Takeout/Google Photos/Photos from 2025/20250716_064034.jpg`

`Not just before and after. Show me what was actually changing.`

## What Not To Do

- do not use the facial treatment images in the core founder transformation carousel
- do not use group/family photos as the main founder story visual
- do not end the founder story on a generic travel shot
- do not use only the strongest lean photo without the real before
