# Marketing Photo Shortlist

This file is a first-pass shortlist from the extracted Google Photos archive under:

- `marketing/fitness_blogging/my_past_photos/extracted/Takeout/Google Photos/`

Selection goal:

- founder transformation story
- weekly progress / check-in proof
- founder credibility
- supporting lifestyle visuals for blogs, SNS, and related marketing

Important notes:

- The strongest marketing material is in `2024` and `2025`.
- `2026` is much stronger for polished founder credibility and lifestyle than for hard physique proof.
- Some burst clusters contain multiple similar images. In those cases, only the strongest frame is listed here.
- A few files that looked promising by filename turned out not to be relevant after image review.
