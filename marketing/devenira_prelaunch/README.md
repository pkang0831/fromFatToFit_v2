# Devenira Prelaunch Kit

This folder turns the prelaunch marketing plan into working assets for a solo founder.

Files:

- `positioning.md`
  Core market position, ICP, headline options, and what the product is not.

- `message_house.md`
  The messaging system for landing pages, social posts, interviews, and future ads.

- `offer_and_funnel.md`
  The single prelaunch offer, CTA decision, and how traffic should move.

- `content_engine_2week.md`
  Two weeks of founder-led content drafts across Instagram, blog, and founder story.

- `manual_outreach_playbook.md`
  Outreach targets, scripts, interview prompts, and tracking structure.

- `prelaunch_kpi_scorecard.md`
  The minimum KPI stack and weekly review process for prelaunch.

Operating rule:

Use the same promise everywhere:

`Weekly AI body check-ins that turn body-change anxiety into visible proof.`
