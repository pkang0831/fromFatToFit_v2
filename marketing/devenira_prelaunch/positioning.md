# Positioning

## One-line Position
Devenira is a `weekly AI body check-in app` for people who are changing their body but cannot trust the mirror or the scale alone.

## Short Version
You upload one body photo, set a direction, and come back weekly to build visual proof that your body is changing.

## The Problem
People do not quit because nothing is happening.

They quit because:

- the mirror changes too slowly
- the scale is noisy
- motivation collapses before proof appears
- most fitness apps collect data without showing a believable story

## The Emotional Promise
Devenira helps users judge their progress with proof instead of panic.

## Primary ICP

### Best First User
- 25-45 years old
- trying to cut body fat or visibly tighten up
- has dieted before and lost confidence in the middle
- is willing to upload photos if privacy is handled clearly
- wants simple weekly guidance, not a giant fitness dashboard

### Good Secondary User
- wedding, trip, photoshoot, or event-driven body goal
- already logging food or workouts but does not feel progress clearly
- wants a weekly routine that feels emotionally stabilizing

## Not For
- people looking only for bodybuilding programming
- people who want a calorie counter only
- users expecting medical-grade body composition data
- users who do not want to use photos at all

## Why This Product Wins
- body change is easier to believe when it is visual
- weekly cadence is easier to sustain than daily obsession
- founder proof gives the product emotional legitimacy
- AI gives interpretation, not just storage

## What To Say
- `Weekly AI body check-ins`
- `Proof over panic`
- `Track the change your mirror misses`
- `One scan is a number. Weekly scans are proof.`
- `Built for the weeks when motivation breaks`

## What Not To Say
- `All-in-one fitness super app`
- `Medical-grade body composition platform`
- `The best calorie tracker`
- `AI wellness ecosystem`
- `Transform instantly`

## Landing Headline Options

### Option 1
Track the change your mirror misses.

Subhead:
Join the first beta users of a weekly AI body check-in app built to turn body-change anxiety into visible proof.

### Option 2
One scan is a number. Weekly check-ins are proof.

Subhead:
Devenira helps you build a visual record of change so you do not have to guess whether the process is working.

### Option 3
Built for the week you want to quit.

Subhead:
Start with one body check-in, come back weekly, and let the visual trend speak before panic does.

## Positioning Test
If someone asks what Devenira is, the answer should fit in one breath:

`It is a weekly AI body check-in app that helps you see progress before your mirror or mood catches up.`
