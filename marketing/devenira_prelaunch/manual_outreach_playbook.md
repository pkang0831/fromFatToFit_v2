# Manual Outreach Playbook

Goal:

Get the first real conversations with people who already feel the problem.

Do not try to close strangers.
Try to learn the language they use when they describe the hard middle of body change.

## Outreach Targets

### Ideal Personas
1. repeat dieter who loses confidence mid-cut
2. wedding or trip deadline user
3. gym user who wants visual proof more than more macros
4. progress-photo avoider who still wants reassurance
5. person frustrated by scale volatility

### Channel Buckets
- small Instagram creators in fat-loss / body-change space
- Reddit threads about scale frustration, dieting doubt, and body-fat tracking
- body transformation Discord or private communities
- comments under honest fitness transformation content
- friends-of-friends who are actively cutting

## 10 Community Types To Enter
1. weight-loss accountability groups
2. body recomposition discussion groups
3. fitness creator comment sections
4. wedding-prep communities
5. travel / beach deadline communities
6. intermittent-fasting groups where scale anxiety shows up
7. progress-photo subreddit threads
8. personal transformation journaling spaces
9. small Discords around cutting
10. creator-led email communities where readers reply personally

## DM Script 01
Use when the person clearly talks about slow progress.

`Hey — I am building a small beta for people who struggle with the part of dieting where the mirror still looks the same even though the effort is real. It is basically a weekly AI body check-in flow built around proof over panic. You came to mind because of [specific reason]. If that problem sounds familiar, I would love to show you the beta and ask what feels useful or useless about it.`

## DM Script 02
Use when the person is a creator or coach.

`Hey — I am building an early beta around weekly body check-ins for people who lose confidence in the slow middle of a cut. Not trying to sell you anything. I would genuinely value your take on whether this solves a real emotional problem or just sounds clever on paper. If you are open, I can send a short link and 3 questions.`

## DM Script 03
Use when the person is a warm contact.

`I am testing something for the exact phase where progress is happening but it is hard to trust. If I send you the beta landing + try flow, would you tell me honestly whether it feels useful or confusing? I do not need politeness. I need the harsh version.`

## Community Post Script
`Question for anyone who has ever nearly quit a cut because the mirror looked the same: would a weekly body check-in app that stores proof over time actually help, or would you still ignore it when panic hits? I am building a beta around that exact problem and want brutal feedback before I overbuild it.`

## 8 Interview Questions
1. What usually makes you doubt your progress?
2. Do you trust the mirror, the scale, photos, or none of them?
3. What happens emotionally after a bad weigh-in?
4. Have you ever stopped because progress felt invisible?
5. Would you upload body photos if privacy were explained clearly?
6. What would make a weekly check-in feel worth returning to?
7. What part of this idea sounds weak or unbelievable?
8. If this worked well, what would change in your behavior?

## What To Capture After Every Call
- exact phrases they used
- what they trusted and did not trust
- objections about privacy
- objections about AI accuracy
- whether weekly cadence felt natural
- whether they asked for food/workout features before believing the core loop

## Red Flags
- they only care about macros and not visual proof
- they want clinical precision
- they refuse photos entirely
- they think weekly is still too frequent

## Success Metric
After 10 conversations, you should know:
- the phrase they use for the hard middle
- the strongest landing headline
- the biggest trust objection
- whether `Join the beta` or `Start one check-in` converts better
