What should I do the day after drinking?

First, please drink more water than usual. You need to drink at least 2 liters or more.

Alcohol dehydrates the body, which causes a feeling of thirst.

People can obtain moisture not only from water but also from food.

There are quite a few cases where thirst is mistaken for hunger.

The next thing to do is exercise.
