The final factor by which cortisol negatively affects dieting is muscle loss.

Cortisol retrieves glucose from various parts of the body to raise blood sugar levels.

Some of them break down muscle to obtain it. This process leads to muscle loss.

Muscles that are perceived as unnecessary due to low usage are broken down.

When you place a strong load on your entire body through exercise, it makes the muscles recognize that they are needed.
