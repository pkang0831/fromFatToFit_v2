# Devenira 14-Day Marketing Execution Plan

## Goal
Make Devenira discoverable and understandable as one product:

**"Think you changed? Let Devenira check it."**

The next 14 days are not for adding more product breadth. They are for:
- fixing indexing signals
- pushing one message everywhere
- publishing search-intent content
- turning content into repeatable distribution

## Core Positioning
Use this message consistently across landing pages, social posts, blog CTAs, and bios.

- Primary line:
  - `Think you changed? Let Devenira check it.`
- Product description:
  - `A weekly body check-in app that helps you verify change with photos, not scale panic.`
- Supporting truth:
  - `The scale lies. The mirror swings. Weekly proof is steadier.`

## Success Metrics
Track only these during the 14-day sprint:

1. Indexed pages in Google Search Console
2. Organic impressions
3. Blog post clicks from Google
4. Landing page to signup CTR
5. Signup to first check-in conversion

## Day 1: Fix Discovery Signals
- Confirm the canonical host is consistent everywhere
- Confirm sitemap uses the same canonical host
- Confirm robots points to the same sitemap host
- Remove future-dated blog posts from public feeds and discovery surfaces
- Re-submit sitemap in Google Search Console
- Inspect and request indexing for:
  - homepage
  - blog index
  - top 5 published blog posts

## Day 2: Tighten Landing Message
- Keep the hero direct and non-poetic
- One landing promise only:
  - `Track weekly body change with photo check-ins, not scale panic.`
- Make sure primary CTA points to first check-in / first scan
- Make sure secondary CTA points to the blog, not a vague product explainer

## Day 3: Publish 2 Search-Intent Posts
Write and publish posts for real search queries:
- `why am i not losing weight after 3 months`
- `scale not moving but clothes fit looser`

Rules:
- answer the question in the first 100 words
- one clear H1
- strong internal links to `/blog`, `/try`, and related posts
- CTA:
  - `Track it with a weekly check-in`

## Day 4: Internal Linking Pass
- Update the top 10 existing blog posts
- Add 3-5 relevant internal links per post:
  - related query post
  - topic page
  - `/try`
  - `/blog`

Priority clusters:
- scale
- mirror
- plateau
- appetite
- body-composition

## Day 5: X Distribution System
For each live post:
- 1 hook tweet
- 1 short thread
- 1 quote/reframe post

Template:
- hook
- one hard truth
- one example
- link to article

Post theme should stay consistent:
- scale panic
- mirror distortion
- weekly proof
- body change without daily emotional guessing

## Day 6: Medium Syndication Pass
- Re-publish or import top 3 strongest search-intent posts to Medium
- Link back clearly to the Devenira site and blog
- Keep titles search-intent-first, not brand-first

## Day 7: Search Console Review
Check:
- `Discovered - currently not indexed`
- `Crawled - currently not indexed`
- `Duplicate, Google chose different canonical`

Use the result to decide whether the issue is:
- content quality
- crawl frequency
- canonical confusion

## Day 8: Publish 2 More Search Posts
Topics:
- `why do i look fatter after starting a diet`
- `how to tell if your body is changing without the scale`

## Day 9: Community Distribution
Write helpful, non-spammy answers for:
- Reddit
- Quora
- niche fitness/body-image communities

Rules:
- answer first
- mention Devenira only when it truly fits
- link to the most relevant article, not always the homepage

## Day 10: Founder Story Asset
Create one strong founder post and one strong founder thread:
- `I lost 50 kg, but the hardest part was not the start. It was the middle when progress was real but hard to trust.`

Purpose:
- authority
- trust
- memorability

## Day 11: Refresh Top 5 Post Titles
Make titles more query-specific and less essay-like where needed.

Good direction:
- direct question
- direct answer
- body-change language
- scale/mirror/appetite wording

## Day 12: Refresh Metadata
For the top 10 posts:
- title tags
- meta descriptions
- open graph titles
- social descriptions

Goal:
- make search snippets sharper
- make X / Medium previews more clickable

## Day 13: Build a Reusable Distribution Loop
For every new post going forward:

1. Publish on site
2. Add internal links
3. Request indexing
4. Publish 3 X posts
5. Publish 1 Medium version or excerpt
6. Post 1 community answer

This becomes the permanent content loop.

## Day 14: Review and Decide
Look only at the 5 metrics:
- indexed pages
- organic impressions
- blog clicks
- landing CTR
- signup to first check-in conversion

Then decide:
- keep pushing SEO + content
- keep content but rewrite landing
- or narrow further around one use case

## Content Angles to Repeat
- The scale is late
- The mirror is emotional
- Body change needs weekly proof
- Hunger and training distort perception
- You need evidence, not motivation

## Things To Stop Doing
- Do not describe Devenira as a broad AI wellness platform
- Do not lead with luxury, transformation fantasy, or futuristic language
- Do not publish brand essays that ignore search intent
- Do not split the message across too many product ideas

## One-Sentence Rule
If a person asks what Devenira is, the answer should stay this simple:

**Devenira helps you check whether your body is actually changing, using weekly photo check-ins instead of scale panic.**
