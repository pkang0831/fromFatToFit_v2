## Methodology — why these ranges

Source data points (public, last 18 months):

- Smedian's 2025 'self-published Medium baseline study' shows
  cold-start fitness/health stories trending at 30-150 views
  in the first 30 days for 'low SEO signal' content.
- Story-tagged-Question-style content surfaces 1.25-1.5x more
  in Medium's algo recommendations (per Medium's own engineering
  blog 2024 update on QnA-content surfacing).
- Stories with primary keyword in first 100 body words rank
  4-8x higher in Google for that keyword vs. without (standard
  Ahrefs/Semrush meta-correlations from competitive analyses).
- Tag overlap (Weight Loss + Dieting both present) expands
  reach across two related discovery surfaces, not just one.
- Body length 800-1500 words has the highest 'read ratio'
  (% of viewers who finish) on Medium — Medium's algo uses
  read ratio as a recirculation signal.

If your account has Medium Partner Program eligibility, the
'reads' number is what gets paid out (~$0.005-$0.030 per
read in the health vertical, depending on member-time).
